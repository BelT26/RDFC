Reigate Dads FC

Reigate Dads is an ecletic mix of wanna-be and has-been footballing fathers (mostly), from in and around the town of Reigate. Camaraderie and sportsmanship are second only to technical football excellence in Reigate Dads' philosophy.

Inaugurated in September 2016, Reigate Dads play on Thursday evenings at the five-a-side astro-turf stadium at South Park Football Club, Whitehall Lane, RH2 8LG. Reigate Dads play against each other within a league, whose triannual season culminates in the award of a highly prized crystal trophy, often referred to as 'The Perspex Block'. The cornerstone of the competition is 'The Algorithm', a sophisticated function that optimises weekly team selection balance, based on a swathe of player statistics.

Social

Post match interviews, press conferences and awards ceremonies usually occur at the South Park FC Clubhouse, however social missions into Reigate's pubs and restaurants are occasionally undertaken.

Reigate Dads recognise the importance of post match rehydration and members are actively discouraged from leaving after a game until their fluid levels have been sufficiently replenished.
